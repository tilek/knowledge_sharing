All of these tips make sense only if done *genuinely*. If you can't do them sincerely, please don't do it.
Smart people will notice it and it's just bad. 😂 These ideas are meant to work for people that actually love people in general and by default.

- A genuine smile is one of the best things to make the best possible first impression

- Can you find someting you are grateful for?
  Examples:
  - thanking a retail salesman/taxi-driver/waiter etc that they are working so late

- Is there something you like about the person?
  If yes, give a compliment, ideally based on something that is more important
  to them and not something they get constantly get compliments for.

- Is there something you are curious about in this person?
  It works better if are curious in something they care a lot about.
  If it looks like the person wants to talk about it, dig as much as you are actually curious about it
  but stopping before it gets too personal. You will have to feel that boundary by yourself.

  Examples: 
  - their name
  - their accent (this is risky and highly context-dependent, some people may be insecure about their accent, if you feel they are insecure you can actually give them a compliment about it)
  - they have a t-shirt with an anime you don't know about it
  - they have a tattoo in a visible place
